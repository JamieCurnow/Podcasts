# App Info

Find information here about the purpose, brand, and target audience of the app.

## Purpose

A free, open-source podcast app — no ads, no tracking, just pure podcasting.

Podcasts is a privacy-first PWA podcast client. Users subscribe to podcast RSS feeds, browse episodes, and listen with a full-featured player (playback speed, bookmarks, chapters, skip forward/back, queue, sleep timer). All data lives locally on-device — there's no account, no server-side user data, and no analytics. It supports offline listening via episode downloads, OPML import/export for moving subscriptions to/from other apps, play history tracking, listening statistics, and episode sharing via the Web Share API.

## Brand / style

- **Name:** Podcasts
- **Tagline:** "For the love of pods"
- **Logo:** SVG, dark and light variants (`/logo-light-no-bg.svg`, `/logo-dark-no-bg.svg`). Displayed rotated -90° in the top nav.
- **Tone:** Warm, casual, no-nonsense. "No ads. No tracking. Just pure podcasting goodness." — that's the voice. Friendly but not fussy.
- **Aesthetic:** Clean, minimal, mobile-first. Neutral palette (no bold brand colours in the UI). Full dark mode support. Noto Sans typeface.
- **Open source ethos:** GitHub-first. The settings page links to the repo and sponsor links (Buy Me a Coffee, GitHub Sponsors). Unapologetically indie.

## Target Audience

Podcast listeners who want a clean, private, no-frills experience. People who are tired of podcast apps that track them, push recommendations, or require accounts. Power users who know what RSS is and don't mind adding feeds manually or via OPML. Mobile-first — designed as a PWA for phones.

## User Stories

- As a listener, I want to subscribe to podcast RSS feeds so I can follow the shows I care about.
- As a listener, I want to browse a chronological feed of new episodes from all my subscriptions so I know what's new.
- As a listener, I want to play an episode with controls for speed, skip forward/back, and seek so I can listen the way I like.
- As a listener, I want to bookmark a moment in an episode with optional notes so I can return to it later.
- As a listener, I want to queue episodes so they play back-to-back automatically.
- As a listener, I want to download episodes for offline listening.
- As a listener, I want to import/export my subscriptions as OPML so I can move between apps without losing my feeds.
- As a listener, I want chapters displayed for episodes that support them so I can jump to sections.
- As a listener, I want the app to remember where I was in every episode so I can pick up where I left off.
- As a listener, I want a sleep timer so the podcast stops playing after a set time or at the end of the episode.
- As a listener, I want to see my play history so I can revisit episodes I've listened to.
- As a listener, I want listening statistics so I can see how much I've listened and my habits over time.
- As a listener, I want to share a podcast or episode with friends via my device's share sheet.
- As a listener, I want the app to work as an installable PWA with lock screen controls so it feels like a native app.
